About Us

Vision Jeune Pour Agir (VJA) is a nonprofit organization based in the Fizi Territory of South Kivu, Democratic Republic of Congo. Founded in 2021, our mission is to improve the livelihoods and well-being of local communities through sustainable development programs.

Our Programs

Agriculture

We support farmers with training, inputs and market access to boost crop yields and incomes.

Livestock

Our livestock projects provide animals, veterinary care and husbandry skills to enhance nutrition and earnings.

Vocational Training

We offer courses in soap-making, breadmaking, sewing and other income-generating trades.
